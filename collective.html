Cutting my teeth as an intern in one of America's most complex industries

Help retool Collective Health's iOS and Android apps with a beautiful and functional design system that scales elegantly with the company.

Role

Product Design Intern

About

In May 2018, I joined Collective Health as a product design intern on the newly formed Design Language System (DLS) team. I was fortunate enough to work on an early-stage design system still in the throes of becoming established in a rapidly expanding startup company. I contributed to products ranging from mobile apps to internal operational tools.

My longest-term project was to help develop a design system for the iOS and mobile apps, which previously had no system in place. I learned a ton about what makes a beautiful UI, and the exciting crossovers between visual and UX design. Huge thanks to Tal Benisty, the senior mobile designer, and Kavita Mistry, my DLS mentor.
