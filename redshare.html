Red Share Installation

An installation & Google Chrome extension exploring our Internet-fueled paranoia

Premise
Inspired by Russian-fueled cultural paranoia, I created an interactive gallery piece that amplifies distortion and questions truth in the social media age.

Role

Artist
Javascript Facebook Google Chrome

About

Gallery visitors are invited to sit down, log in to their Facebook account, and witness a total distortion of their newsfeed, messages, and even their own profiles. A Google Chrome extension injects hateful and disturbing rhetoric into friend's posts, and images are replaced with 1980s cultural and political relics. The browser window wobbles and glitches at random, violently streaking the screen with flashes of red. The CRT monitor produces a piercing high-pitched whine that fills the room.

Red Share was exhibited in March 2018 at the Visual Arts Facility at UC San Diego. Thanks to Amy Alexander and Pinar Yoldas for their guidance.
